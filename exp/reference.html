Great, you are ready to play!

In this game, you will be playing with a partner and your goal is to get them to guess the object on the left side of the screen. If they get it right, you will earn points.

There are two ways you can send the message:



- Label (10 points): Type out the name of the item to send to your partner. If you send the right label, you get 10 points!

- Click (3 points): Or, click on the target object directly and send that selection to your partner for 3 points!

If your partner chooses the wrong object based on your message, you will get 0 points.

Before you start, we want to make sure you know the rules for our game!



How many points do you get if you click on the target object?

Not quite, you will receive 3 points for clicking!


How many points do you get if you type the correct label for the object?

Actually, you will receive 10 points for using the right label!


How many points do you get if you type the incorrect label for the object?

In fact, you will receive 0 points if your label is wrong!
