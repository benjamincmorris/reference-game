After you click the button below, you will begin the game. Remember the goal of this game is to be able to get another player to correctly select one of the nine objects based on a message you send. Each round, you will earn points if the other player correctly identifies the target object, but just how many points you will earn depends on the type of message you send.



- Label (10 points): You can type out the name of the item to send to your partner. If you correctly label the object, and your partner correctly understands the label, you will recieve 10 points.

- Click (3 points): Or, you can instead choose to click on the target object in an array showing all 9 objects and send that selection to your partner. With this strategy, you will receive 3 points when your partner correctly identifies the target obect.
