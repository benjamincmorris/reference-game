After you click the button below, you will begin the game. Remember the goal of this game is to be able to get another player to correctly select one of the nine objects. Each round of the game, you will be shown one target object and allowed to send a message to the other player. if the other player is able to correctly identify the target object based on your message, you will be given 10 points. There are two types of messages that you can send:



- Label: You can type out the name of the item to send to your partner. If you correctly label the object, and your partner correctly understands the label, you will recieve all 10 points.

- Click: Or, you can instead choose to click on the target object in an array showing all 9 objects and send that selection to your partner. However, you will be charged 7 points for using this method, and will thus only receive 3 points when your partner correctly identifies the target obect.
